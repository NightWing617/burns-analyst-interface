About BurnDx

Our Mission

BurnDx aims to revolutionize burn care by providing immediate, accurate AI-powered burn assessments to healthcare professionals. Our technology bridges the gap between initial injury and specialized care, helping clinicians make faster, more precise decisions to improve patient outcomes.

Faster Diagnosis

Cut assessment time from hours to minutes

Improved Accuracy

AI trained on thousands of verified cases

Better Outcomes

Optimized treatment plans for recovery

How BurnDx Works

1

Capture & Upload

Using a smartphone or tablet, capture images of the burn wound from multiple angles following our imaging guidelines. The app automatically anonymizes patient data before upload.

2

AI Analysis

Our proprietary deep learning algorithms analyze the wound characteristics - depth, area, color patterns, and tissue damage indicators - comparing against thousands of validated cases.

3

Clinical Insights

BurnDx generates a comprehensive clinical report with depth assessment, TBSA calculation, and essential clinical flags that guide next steps in patient management.

4

Treatment Guidance

Get evidence-based recommendations for fluid resuscitation, topical treatments, referral needs, and long-term management based on burn characteristics and patient factors.

Ethics & Privacy

Privacy-First Design

At BurnDx, we believe privacy is fundamental to building trust in healthcare technology. Our architecture implements end-to-end protection ensuring patient confidentiality while providing clinical value.

Data De-identification

All patient identifiers are automatically removed before processing. Facial features in images are obscured using advanced techniques.

Encryption Standards

Bank-level AES-256 encryption for data at rest and in transit. Strict key management with hardware security modules.

Local Processing Options

On-premise deployment available for institutions requiring data to remain entirely within their network infrastructure.

Automatic Deletion

Original images are deleted after analysis unless specifically retained by clinicians. Audit trails track all data interactions.

Ethical AI Principles

Bias Mitigation

Our models are trained on diverse, globally-sourced datasets to minimize demographic bias. Regular audits evaluate performance across skin tones and ethnicities.

Transparency

Clinicians receive not just predictions but visual explanations showing which wound features contributed to the assessment. Confidence metrics accompany all outputs.

Clinical Oversight

BurnDx is designed as a clinical decision support tool, not a replacement for physician judgment. All critical findings require clinician verification.
